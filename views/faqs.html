Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is a katana?
Ans. A katana is a traditional Japanese sword characterized by its curved, slender blade and long grip. It is one of the most iconic and revered swords in Japanese history.
Q2. Are your katanas authentic and traditional?
Ans. Yes, we pride ourselves on offering authentic and traditional katanas. Our swords are handcrafted by skilled artisans using traditional techniques and materials.
Q3. What types of katanas do you offer?
Ans. We offer a wide range of katanas, including various blade lengths, styles, and designs. Our collection includes practical katanas for martial arts practice, decorative katanas for display, and functional katanas for collectors.
Q4. Are the blades sharp?
Ans. Yes, our functional katanas have sharp blades suitable for cutting exercises or traditional use. However, please exercise caution and handle them responsibly.
Q5. Can I customize the katana?
Ans. Depending on the specific product, customization options may be available. Contact our customer support for inquiries about customization options such as blade engraving or handle wrapping.
Q6. How do I care for my katana?
Ans. Proper care is essential to maintain the quality and longevity of your katana. We recommend regular cleaning, oiling the blade, and storing it in a dry and secure place. Detailed care instructions are provided with each purchase.
Q7. Is it legal to own a katana?
Ans. The legalities surrounding katana ownership vary by jurisdiction. It's important to familiarize yourself with the local laws and regulations regarding sword ownership in your area.
Q8. Do you ship internationally?
Ans. Yes, we offer international shipping. However, please note that some countries may have restrictions or regulations on the importation of bladed weapons. Ensure compliance with your local customs laws before placing an order.
Q9. What is your return policy?
Ans. We have a flexible return policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer support within a specified timeframe for instructions on returns or exchanges.
Q10. How long does shipping take?
Ans. Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method chosen at checkout. We strive to process orders promptly, and an estimated delivery timeframe will be provided during the checkout process.
